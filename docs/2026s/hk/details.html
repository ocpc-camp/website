Detailed Information

Where is the camp held?

The camp will be held at City University of Hong Kong (CityU), in Kowloon Tong.

How to get there?

  • By MTR: City University of Hong Kong is right next to Kowloon Tong station. Take the MTR East Rail line (or Kwun Tong line) to Kowloon Tong; the campus is connected to the Festival Walk mall directly above the station.
  • By plane: Hong Kong International Airport (HKG) connects to the city centre via the Airport Express and onward MTR; the trip to Kowloon Tong takes well under an hour.

Where to stay?

We recommend staying at the on-campus CityUHK Lodge, a short walk from the contest venue. Room types and rates will be confirmed here closer to the camp. Onsite teams can ask us to reserve a room on their behalf when registering — just choose the CityU Lodge option on the registration form and give your check-in / check-out dates. Teams cover their own accommodation costs.

Food

Please note that food (other than coffee breaks) and accommodation are not included in the participation fee.

We will provide snacks near the contest area during contest days. There are many dining options on and around the CityU campus, including the Festival Walk mall above Kowloon Tong station.

How to register for the camp?

Register using the registration form (pick "Hong Kong — onsite" in it). Onsite capacity is limited, and applications for onsite participation are processed on a first-come-first-served basis, so please register early. If you require a visa or entry permit to visit Hong Kong, register as soon as possible and let us know — see the contact page if you have any questions.
