Registration & fees

Participation is 198€ per team. Registration is through a single shared form for all OCPC Summer 2026 sites — it will be announced soon; join our Discord or contact us to be notified.

The fee, the early-bird / hardship / Ukraine discounts and what it covers are the same across all sites — see registration & participation fee on the home page.
