Detailed Information

Where is the camp held?

The camp will be held at the Faculty of Computer Science, Alexandru Ioan Cuza University of Iași (UAIC), in the Copou area of Iași — a green, leafy part of the city dotted with parks and university buildings. UAIC is the oldest university in Romania and home to the country's oldest Computer Science faculty. You can see the exact faculty location on Google Maps.

How to get there?

  • By plane: Iași has an international airport, Iași International Airport (IAS), with connections to several European hubs. Iași is in the north-east of Romania and is one of the country's largest cities. From the airport, the Copou campus is a short taxi or ride-share trip.
  • By train or coach: Iași is connected by rail and coach to Bucharest and other Romanian cities. The central railway station is a few kilometres from the campus.

Where to stay?

Several hotels and hostels are within about 1 km of the faculty, in the Copou area:

  • Prestige / Residence Copou
  • Iassium Residence
  • Hostel Andrei

Many more options are available a little further out.

Food

Please note that food (other than coffee breaks) and accommodation are not included in the participation fee.

We will provide snacks near the contest area during contest days. There are plenty of restaurants and cafés in the Copou area and the nearby city centre.

What to see in Iași

Iași is the historical capital of Moldavia and a lively university city. For ideas on what to visit and walking routes around the city, see iasi.travel.

How to register for the camp?

Registration details will be announced soon. Contact us or join our Discord to be notified. If you require a visa, please register as soon as registration opens, as it might take a few weeks to receive (depending on your location).
