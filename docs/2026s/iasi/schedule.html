Schedule

The camp runs 12–20 September 2026: 7 ICPC-style contests with 2 days off in between.

All times are in EEST (UTC +3). Online participants have the opportunity to start the contest in a 24h window, starting anytime after the start of the original contest. Schedule may be subject to change, please follow us on Discord for most up to date info.
