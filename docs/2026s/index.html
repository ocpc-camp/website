What is this?

OCPC is a camp to prepare students for ICPC and similar programming competitions. It is aimed at the highest level of competition, i.e. teams who wish to show strong results in ICPC regionals and participate seriously in ICPC World Finals. Each edition consists of a series of ICPC-style contests (5 hours, 9–12 problems each) for 3-person teams, with analysis sessions and days off in between.

The camp was founded in 2023 and has since run editions in Osijek and other host cities. Browse all past editions, contest mirrors and standings in the archive.

Who can participate?

Participation is open to anyone, but the camp is primarily aimed at ICPC-eligible teams representing universities. You compete in teams of up to three; the participation fee is charged per team rather than per person, so a fuller team pays less per participant.

Participation fee

The participation fee for both online and onsite participants is 198€ per team (the same for all sites). As in previous editions, it does not include regular meals, travel or accommodation.

Discounts
  • Early bird discount: Reduced fee of 147€ per team if you register before the early-bird deadline (to be announced).
  • Hardship discount: Fee reduction to 48€ per team, on request, if the default fee creates a financial burden for you. We do not require any proof or documentation, and trust your self-assessment. Nevertheless, we ask you to be mindful when using this option, as we rely on participation fees to run the camp.
  • Ukraine support: We support and empathize with those affected by the ongoing war in Ukraine, and offer free participation for affected individuals and teams affiliated with Ukrainian institutions.

Individual sites may add their own arrangements (for example, free participation for local teams) — see each camp page.
