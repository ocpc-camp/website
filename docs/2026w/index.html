What is this?

This is a camp to prepare students for ICPC and similar programming competitions. The camp is aimed at the highest level of competition, i.e. teams who wish to show strong results in ICPC regionals and participate seriously in ICPC World Finals.

The camp consists of 7 ICPC-style contests, i.e. 5 hours and 9-12 problems each, and 2 days off. The contests are intended for 3-person teams.

Who can participate?

Participation is open to anyone, but the camp is primarily aimed at ICPC-eligible teams representing universities. Participants need to be registered in teams. To register, please fill the registration form.

Registration deadlines: January 17, 2026 for remote participation, January 10, 2026 for onsite participation in Osijek. If you require a visa, please register as soon as possible.

Participation Fee

The participation fee for both online and onsite participants is 198€ per team.

Similar to previous camp editions, the fee does not include regular meals, travel or accommodation.

Discounts
  • Early bird discount: Reduced fee of 147€ per team if you register before December 14, 2025.
  • Hardship discount: Fee reduction to 99€ per team, on request, if the default fee creates a financial burden for you. We do not require any proof or documentation, and trust your self-assessment. Nevertheless, we ask you to be mindful when using this option, as we rely on participation fees to run the camp.
  • Ukraine support: We support and empathize with those affected by the ongoing war in Ukraine, and offer free participation for affected individuals and teams affiliated with Ukrainian institutions.
